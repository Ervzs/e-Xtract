e-Xtract

e-Xtract aims to solve the ever-growing problem of electronic waste in the Philippines. By giving you the ability to disassemble your e-waste and find valuable parts, our environment can potentially become a toxic and hazard-free one. We'll also give you the proper steps on how to safely dispose of your e-waste.
